Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2025

Introduction

ScanX ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Camera Access: We access your device camera solely to scan documents, QR codes, and barcodes. Images are processed locally on your device.
  • Storage Access: We access your device storage to save scanned documents and PDFs locally on your device.
  • Google Drive Access: If you choose to enable Google Drive sync, we access your Google Drive to backup and sync your scanned documents. We only access files created by our app in a dedicated folder.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide document scanning functionality
  • To save and organize your scanned documents
  • To sync your documents with Google Drive (only if you enable this feature)
  • To improve our app and user experience

Data Storage and Security

Your scanned documents are stored locally on your device. If you enable Google Drive sync, your documents are also stored in your personal Google Drive account. We do not store your documents on our servers.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Drive API: For optional cloud backup and sync functionality
  • Google Sign-In: For authentication when using Google Drive features
  • Firebase Analytics: For anonymous usage analytics to improve our app
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For crash reporting to improve app stability

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
  • Disconnect Google Drive sync at any time
  • Opt out of analytics (through device settings)

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
